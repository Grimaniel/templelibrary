IDownState
 
Packagetemple.ui.states.down
Interfacepublic interface IDownState extends IState , IEnableable , IDisplayObject , IShowable , IEventDispatcher , IBitmapDrawable
Implementors DownFadeState

A DisplayObject that implements IDownState represents the down state of his parent.

A DisplayObject is in 'down state' when the Mouse hovers the DisplayObject and the button is down.
