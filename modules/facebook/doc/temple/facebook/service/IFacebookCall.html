Interfacepublic interface IFacebookCall extends IPendingCall , IDestructible , ICoreEventDispatcher , ILoader , ICancellable , IEventDispatcher , IDestructible

Object containing information about a call on the FacebookAPI and FacebookService.

This object dispatches a COMPLETE event when the call is completed. It also contains the data which is returned from Facebook.
